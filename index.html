Welcome to Cloud 9 Café, where every visit feels like a getaway to your happy place. Nestled in the heart of Billericay, Essex, our café is the perfect spot to unwind, enjoy exceptional coffee, and indulge in delicious food.

Visit Us Today!


Welcome to Cloud 9, your local haven in Billericay, Essex. We pride ourselves on serving the finest coffee and delicious homemade treats in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Whether you're catching up with friends, need a quiet spot to work, or just want to indulge in a little 'me time', Cloud 9 is the perfect place to relax and rejuvenate.


Cloud 9 Cafe & Coffee Bar was born out of a love for exceptional coffee and a passion for creating a welcoming space for the community. Our journey began in Billericay, and we are proud to serve our local residents with the best in coffee and hospitality. Come in and experience the difference that a little bit of heart can make
